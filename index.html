About Me

G'day! I'm Seb, and I've spent the last 15 years on a mission to prove that enterprise software doesn't have to feel like... well, enterprise software. You know the type - clunky, confusing, and designed by someone who clearly never has to use it.

My journey started with a Bachelor of Interactive Entertainment in Animation (yes, really!), which probably explains why I'm obsessed with making business software that people actually want to use. After stints in game QA and running a photo centre, I discovered my calling: solving complex business problems with elegant tech solutions.

These days, I'm the General Manager at Octfolio, where we help organisations across Australia and New Zealand manage hazardous materials compliance without wanting to tear their hair out. I've had the privilege of working with everyone from poultry producers (saving them $1M+ annually) to power companies (96% error reduction!), always with the same goal: making their work lives easier.

I'm particularly proud of building remote teams before it was trendy (been doing it since 2016!), creating a partner program that help drive revenue, and most importantly, fostering a culture where my team genuinely enjoys Monday mornings. That last one might be my greatest achievement.

What Drives Me

  • People Over Process: Great teams build great products, not the other way around
  • Elegant Solutions: The best answer is usually the simplest one that actually works
  • Remote-First: Talent is everywhere, so why limit yourself to one postcode?
  • Family Time is Sacred: I block out 7-9am and 7-8pm for family - no exceptions
